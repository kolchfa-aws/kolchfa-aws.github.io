OpenSearch Project Roadmap 2024–2025

OpenSearch is a rapidly growing open-source product suite comprising a search engine, an ingestion system, language clients, and a user interface for analytics. OpenSearch contributors and maintainers are innovating in all these areas at a fast pace. To steer the project’s development effectively, we have revamped the project roadmap to provide better transparency into both short- and long-term enhancements. In this blog post, we are excited to share the new theme-based, community-driven OpenSearch Project Roadmap for 2024–2025.
